## ðŸ“Œ What is SAP?

**SAP** is an **ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)** software used by organizations to manage their core business processes in a centralized and integrated system.

SAP helps manage:
- Finance & Accounting
- Sales & Customer Management
- Supply Chain & Logistics
- Human Resources
- Manufacturing & Production
